Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 3, 2026

1. Our Core Promise

ANONYMITY FIRST

We do not verify who you are. We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or precise GPS locations.

WE NEVER
  • Use data for advertising
  • Sell data to third parties
  • Profile or track you across apps

2. Data We Collect

We use Google Firebase to collect minimal technical data strictly for app stability.

Crash Reporting

Stack traces and device state (e.g., "Pixel 6, Android 13") to help us fix bugs.

Usage Analytics

Interaction events (e.g., "Played: Episode Name", "Search: Query") to identify trending content. Approximate location (City/Country) derived from IP address.

3. Control & Deletion

You can toggle all data collection in Settings.

Right to Erasure

You can request immediate permanent deletion of your data.

  1. Go to Settings > Data Management
  2. Email us your Instance ID

Retention Policy

  • Auto-Deletion: User-level data is automatically deleted after 14 months of inactivity.
  • Active Use: If you continue to use the app, the retention period resets to preserve your preferences.
